Clever Spend

A personal finance app designed to simplify expense tracking and budgeting. It helps users to take control of their finances through intuitive design and user-friendly features.

View website

Platform

iOS and Android

Timeline

June 2024 - Ian 2025

Role

Co-founder & UX Engineer

Team

The Problem

Tracking expenses across multiple categories can be overwhelming, especially without a clear financial overview. Budgeting tools are often complex, unintuitive, and lack features like automatic bank connections, making money management difficult.

Competitive Research

To guide the development of Clever Spend, I analyzed 8 popular personal finance apps. This research focused on key aspects like core features, usability, design, and differentiators, providing valuable insights to ensure Clever Spend stands out in the market.

*To protect the integrity of the business, only a portion of the collected research is displayed.

Findings

  • Prioritizing simplicity and usability, avoiding feature bloat.
  • Incorporating visual insights like graphs and charts to enhance expense tracking and clarity.
  • Ensuring the inclusion of essential features found in top-performing apps.
