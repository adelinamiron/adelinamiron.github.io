InstaScribe

InstaScribe is an iOS app that helps users create perfect captions for their social media posts effortlessly. Powered by AI technology, it generates captions tailored to the user's tone, style, and preferences.

Platform

iOS

Timeline

Feb 2023 - May 2023

Role

UX Designer

Team

1 UX Designer & 1 Developer

The Problem

Creating the perfect caption for social media posts is a common challenge for users. Many struggle to find the right words to match their tone or express their message effectively.

Additionally, users face difficulties in adapting captions to different styles, such as humorous, professional, or sentimental, and balancing message length with impact.
